# Shia LaBeouf "Just Do It" Motivational Speech (Original Video by LaBeouf, Rönkkö & Turner)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0

## Tone

An unrelenting, almost confrontational motivational speech — raw energy delivered straight to camera with zero subtlety.

## Notable Quotes

> "Don't let your dreams be dreams." — [[0:05]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=5) — *Reframes dreams as something you control, not something that happens to you*

> "Yesterday you said tomorrow." — [[0:08]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=8) — *Calls out the exact pattern of self-sabotage most people recognise in themselves*

> "Just do it!" — [[0:15]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=15) — *The thesis of the entire speech, stripped to its minimum*

> "Some people dream of success, while you're gonna wake up and work hard at it." — [[0:25]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=25) — *Separates dreamers from doers in one sentence*

> "Nothing is impossible." — [[0:45]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=45) — *Closes the speech by removing every remaining excuse*

## Most Shareable Quote
