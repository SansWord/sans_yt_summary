# Shia LaBeouf "Just Do It" Motivational Speech (Original Video by LaBeouf, Rönkkö & Turner)

## Overview

A high-energy motivational speech by Shia LaBeouf urging viewers to stop procrastinating and pursue their dreams without hesitation. In under a minute, he delivers a relentless call to action — equal parts absurd and genuinely compelling.

- [[0:25]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=25) Some people dream of success; you need to wake up and work for it

- [[0:45]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=45) Nothing is impossible — make your dreams come true

## Takeaways

- Procrastination is the only real obstacle between you and your goals
- Motivation is not something you wait for — it follows action
- The message is simple by design: overthinking is the enemy
