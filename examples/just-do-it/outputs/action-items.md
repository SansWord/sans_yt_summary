# Shia LaBeouf "Just Do It" Motivational Speech (Original Video by LaBeouf, Rönkkö & Turner)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0

## Action Items

- **Stop** waiting for the right moment — it doesn't exist [[0:08]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=8)
- **Do** the thing you said you'd do tomorrow, today [[0:10]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=10)
- **Start** before you feel ready [[0:15]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=15)
- **Work** while others only dream [[0:25]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=25)
- **Push** past the point where you want to quit [[0:35]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=35)
- **Refuse** to accept impossibility as an excuse [[0:45]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXsQAXx_ao0&t=45)

## Core Mindset Shift

Stop treating your dreams as something that will happen someday — treat them as work that starts now.
